A recent [Cognilytica study](https://www.cognilytica.com/document/data-preparation-labeling-for-ai-2020/) states that the greatest challenge faced by most AI/ML teams is data management and optimization. About 50% of their time they spend in developing AI is on training data, while another 15% implies augmenting datasets to optimize processes around training data. In the long run, these optimizations can help them save a significant amount of money and time.
